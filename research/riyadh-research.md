## 1. Market Overview

Saudi Arabia is undergoing a dramatic entertainment transformation under **Vision 2030**. Riyadh, the capital, is at the epicenter of this change — from a conservative city with almost no entertainment to a global events destination.

### Key Statistics
- **Population:** ~8 million (Riyadh Province ~9M)
- **Annual Riyadh Season Visitors:** 15+ million (2023-24)
- **Currency:** Saudi Riyal (SAR) — pegged to USD at 3.75
- **GDP Per Capita:** ~$28,000 USD
- **Expat Population:** ~30% (growing)

### The Entertainment Revolution
| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2016 | General Entertainment Authority (GEA) established |
| 2018 | First cinema opens (35-year ban lifted) |
| 2018 | Women allowed to attend events |
| 2019 | First Riyadh Season festival |
| 2019 | First NYE celebration |
| 2019 | Tourist visas introduced |
| 2024 | 11,400+ entertainment licenses issued |
| 2025+ | Potential alcohol for tourists (discussions) |

### Seasonal Patterns
| Season | Months | Activity |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Riyadh Season** | Oct–Mar | Mega events, festivals, global stars |
| **Shoulder** | Apr–May, Sep | Moderate activity |
| **Summer** | Jun–Aug | Reduced (extreme heat 45°C+) |
| **Ramadan** | Varies | Night events, cultural programs |

### Weekend & Work Week
- **Weekend:** Friday–Saturday
- **Work Week:** Sunday–Thursday
- **Note:** No brunch culture (no alcohol), different rhythm than Dubai

## 2. Areas & Neighborhoods

### Olaya District
- **Vibe:** Commercial hub, business center, dining destination
- **Landmarks:** Kingdom Centre Tower, Al Faisaliah Tower, Tahlia Street
- **Accommodation:** Four Seasons, InterContinental
- **Dining:** High-end restaurants, international chains
- **Entertainment:** Limited — focus is business/dining
- **Best For:** Business travelers, central location
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### KAFD (King Abdullah Financial District)
- **Vibe:** Ultra-modern, futuristic skyline, new development
- **Landmarks:** KAFD towers, Conference Palace
- **Accommodation:** Raffles Riyadh (coming), St. Regis
- **Dining:** VIA Riyadh (Michelin restaurants), Mr. Chow
- **Entertainment:** Events at VIA, concerts
- **Best For:** Modern experience, fine dining
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### Diplomatic Quarter (DQ)
- **Vibe:** Green, quiet, embassy enclave
- **Landmarks:** Embassies, Tuwaiq Palace
- **Accommodation:** Marriott, Sheraton, Crowne Plaza
- **Dining:** Hotel restaurants, cafes
- **Entertainment:** Limited
- **Best For:** Families, quiet stay, business
- **Price Level:** $$$

### Diriyah
- **Vibe:** Historic, UNESCO heritage, Saudi roots
- **Landmarks:** At-Turaif UNESCO site, Bujairi Terrace
- **Accommodation:** Bab Samhan, Diriyah Company hotels (opening)
- **Dining:** Bujairi Terrace restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Heritage events, Diriyah Season
- **Best For:** History lovers, cultural tourism
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### Boulevard District (Hittin Area)
- **Vibe:** Entertainment hub, Riyadh Season epicenter
- **Landmarks:** Boulevard Riyadh City, Boulevard World
- **Accommodation:** Limited (new hotels coming)
- **Dining:** 100+ restaurants in Boulevard
- **Entertainment:** Major events, concerts, zones
- **Best For:** Nightlife, entertainment seekers
- **Price Level:** $$$

### North Riyadh (Al Malqa, Hittin, Al Yasmin)
- **Vibe:** Modern residential, new developments, malls
- **Landmarks:** Al Nakheel Mall, Granada Centre
- **Accommodation:** Mid-range hotels
- **Dining:** Mall dining, cafes
- **Entertainment:** Cinema complexes
- **Best For:** Modern suburban feel
- **Price Level:** $$–$$$

### Old Riyadh / Qasr Al-Hokm
- **Vibe:** Historic core, traditional markets
- **Landmarks:** Masmak Fortress, Souq Al-Zal
- **Accommodation:** Limited
- **Dining:** Traditional restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Heritage experiences
- **Best For:** History, authentic culture
- **Price Level:** $–$$

### JAX District (formerly Riyadh Station)
- **Vibe:** Creative, artsy, warehouse conversion
- **Landmarks:** Art galleries, studios
- **Accommodation:** None (area focus)
- **Dining:** Trendy cafes
- **Entertainment:** Art events, creative scene
- **Best For:** Art lovers, creatives
- **Price Level:** $$

- Locations: Selected malls
- Experiences: Premium seating

### Pricing
- Standard: SAR 50–70
- IMAX/Premium: SAR 80–120
- Suites: SAR 150–250

### Cinema Culture Notes
- **Censorship:** Some scenes cut, ratings enforced
- **Gender Mixing:** Now allowed
- **New Industry:** Only since 2018
- **Target:** 350 cinemas, 2,500 screens by 2030

## 8. Local Knowledge

### Alcohol Laws
**CRITICAL DIFFERENCE FROM DUBAI**

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Status** | Completely prohibited |
| **For Tourists** | No alcohol anywhere |
| **Penalties** | Fines, jail, deportation |
| **Smuggling** | Serious offense |
| **Hotels/Events** | Zero alcohol served |
| **Future** | Some relaxation discussed for 2026+ |

**Note:** February 2026 reports indicate Saudi may allow alcohol for wealthy foreign expats in the Diplomatic Quarter, with potential tourist access in future. Still very restrictive.

### Dress Code

| Setting | Men | Women |
|---------|-----|-------|
| **Public** | Long pants, covered shoulders | Loose clothing, no abaya required (since 2019) |
| **Malls/Events** | Casual acceptable | Modest, no abaya required |
| **Restaurants** | Smart casual | Modest |
| **Religious Sites** | Conservative | Covered head-to-toe |

### Cultural Etiquette
- **Prayer Times:** Shops close 5x daily (15-30 min)
- **Friday:** Extended midday prayer closure
- **Ramadan:** No public eating/drinking in daylight
- **Photography:** Ask permission, avoid women
- **Gender:** Mixing allowed but respect personal space
- **Hospitality:** Accept coffee/dates when offered

### Transport

#### Riyadh Metro (Opened 2024)
- **Lines:** 6 lines, 85 stations
- **Coverage:** Major areas including KAFD, Olaya, Airport
- **Cost:** SAR 4–7

#### Taxis & Ride-Hailing
| App | Notes |
|-----|-------|
| **Uber** | Available, reliable |
| **Careem** | Regional, popular |
| **Jeeny** | Local option |

#### Other
- **Driving:** International license accepted
- **Women Driving:** Legal since 2018
- **Traffic:** Heavy congestion

### Currency & Money
- **Currency:** Saudi Riyal (SAR)
- **Rate:** Fixed at 3.75 SAR = 1 USD
- **Cards:** Widely accepted
- **ATMs:** Abundant
- **Tipping:** Not expected, appreciated

### Language
- **Official:** Arabic
- **English:** Common in business, tourism
- **Signs:** Often bilingual

### Weather
| Month | Temperature | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Nov–Feb | 15–25°C | Best time to visit |
| Mar–Apr | 25–35°C | Pleasant |
| May–Sep | 38–50°C | Extremely hot |
| Oct | 30–38°C | Warming |
