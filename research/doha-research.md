## 1. Market Overview

Doha is Qatar's capital and the most developed city in the country. Post-FIFA World Cup 2022, it has world-class infrastructure, stadiums, and a growing entertainment scene. More liberal than Saudi, alcohol is available at licensed hotels.

### Key Statistics
- **Population:** ~2.4 million (country ~3M)
- **GDP Per Capita:** Highest in the world
- **Currency:** Qatari Riyal (QAR)
- **Character:** Wealthy, modern, post-World Cup boom

### Key Differences from Dubai
| Factor | Dubai | Doha |
|--------|-------|------|
| Size | Larger, more established | Smaller, concentrated |
| Alcohol | Widely available | Hotel-only, more restricted |
| Nightlife | Vibrant, many venues | Limited, hotel-focused |
| Tourism | Mature | Growing post-World Cup |
| Weekend | Fri-Sat | Fri-Sat |

## 2. Areas & Neighborhoods

### Souq Waqif
- **Vibe:** Traditional market, heritage, authentic
- **Landmarks:** Traditional souq, Falcon Souq, heritage hotels
- **Dining:** Traditional restaurants, shisha cafes
- **Entertainment:** Street performances, heritage events
- **Best For:** Culture, shopping, atmosphere
- **Price Level:** $–$$

### The Pearl-Qatar
- **Vibe:** Luxury island, Mediterranean-style
- **Landmarks:** Porto Arabia, Qanat Quartier
- **Accommodation:** St. Regis, Marsa Malaz Kempinski
- **Dining:** Upscale restaurants, waterfront cafes
- **Entertainment:** Yacht harbor, beach clubs, bars
- **Best For:** Luxury, nightlife, dining
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### Lusail City
- **Vibe:** New futuristic city, World Cup legacy
- **Landmarks:** Lusail Stadium, Place Vendôme Mall
- **Accommodation:** New hotels
- **Dining:** Mall dining, new restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Stadium events, modern attractions
- **Best For:** Modern architecture, events
- **Price Level:** $$$

### West Bay
- **Vibe:** Business district, skyline, CBD
- **Landmarks:** Doha Tower, iconic skyscrapers
- **Accommodation:** Sheraton, W Doha, Ritz-Carlton
- **Dining:** Hotel restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Hotel bars, lounges
- **Best For:** Business, skyline views
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### Katara Cultural Village
- **Vibe:** Cultural, artistic, family
- **Landmarks:** Amphitheater, galleries, beach
- **Dining:** Restaurants, cafes
- **Entertainment:** Cultural events, exhibitions
- **Best For:** Art, culture, families
- **Price Level:** $$–$$$

### Msheireb Downtown
- **Vibe:** Smart city, sustainable, heritage-modern
- **Landmarks:** Msheireb Museums
- **Dining:** Modern cafes, restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Cultural, walking

## 8. Local Knowledge

### Transport
- **Metro:** Doha Metro (3 lines, modern)
- **Taxis:** Uber/Careem, Karwa taxis
- **Traffic:** Moderate

### Alcohol
- Hotels and licensed restaurants only
- Very expensive (taxes)
- No public consumption
- 21+ age

### Dress Code
- Modest in public (shoulders/knees)
- More relaxed at hotels
- Traditional souks: conservative

### Cultural Notes
- Small country, high wealth
- Post-World Cup tourism push
- More liberal than Saudi
- Large expat population
- Friday is main prayer day

### Best Time to Visit
- **Nov–Mar:** Cooler, pleasant
- **Summer:** Very hot, humid
