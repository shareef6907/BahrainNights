## 1. Market Overview

Abu Dhabi is the UAE capital — wealthier than Dubai but more conservative and culturally focused. Entertainment is growing but remains more subdued than Dubai. Focus on cultural tourism, family entertainment, and world-class attractions.

### Key Statistics
- **Population:** ~1.5 million (emirate ~3.8M)
- **GDP:** Largest in UAE (oil wealth)
- **Currency:** UAE Dirham (AED)
- **Character:** More conservative, cultural, family-focused than Dubai

### Key Differences from Dubai
| Factor | Dubai | Abu Dhabi |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Nightlife | Vibrant, many venues | More limited, hotel-focused |
| Tourism Focus | Entertainment, shopping | Culture, family, attractions |
| Pace | Fast, 24/7 | More relaxed |
| Alcohol | Widely available | Available but less prevalent |
| Weekend | Fri-Sat | Fri-Sat |

## 2. Areas & Neighborhoods

### Yas Island
- **Vibe:** Entertainment hub, theme parks, events
- **Landmarks:** Ferrari World, Warner Bros World, Yas Waterworld, SeaWorld, Yas Marina Circuit
- **Accommodation:** W Abu Dhabi, Yas Hotel, Hilton, Crowne Plaza
- **Dining:** Theme park dining, hotel restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Major concerts, F1, theme parks
- **Best For:** Families, thrill-seekers, F1 fans
- **Price Level:** $$$

### Saadiyat Island
- **Vibe:** Cultural, luxury beach, artistic
- **Landmarks:** Louvre Abu Dhabi, Manarat Al Saadiyat, future Guggenheim
- **Accommodation:** St. Regis, Park Hyatt, Jumeirah
- **Dining:** Mamsha Al Saadiyat restaurants (Niri, Beirut Sur Mer, Antonia)
- **Entertainment:** Art exhibitions, cultural events
- **Best For:** Culture lovers, beach seekers, luxury travelers
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### Al Maryah Island
- **Vibe:** Business district, upscale shopping, dining
- **Landmarks:** The Galleria, Cleveland Clinic
- **Accommodation:** Four Seasons, Rosewood
- **Dining:** Zuma, Dragon's Tooth, licensed restaurants
- **Entertainment:** High-end bars, hotel lounges
- **Best For:** Business travelers, fine dining
- **Price Level:** $$$$

### The Corniche
- **Vibe:** Waterfront promenade, city center, family beach
- **Landmarks:** Corniche Beach, Emirates Palace, ADNOC HQ
- **Accommodation:** Emirates Palace, St. Regis, Intercontinental
- **Dining:** Hotel restaurants, corniche cafes
- **Entertainment:** Beach activities, sunset walks
- **Best For:** City stay, beach access, iconic hotels
- **Price Level:** $$$–$$$$

### Downtown / Tourist Club Area
- **Vibe:** Central, malls, traditional
- **Landmarks:** Abu Dhabi Mall, World Trade Center
- **Accommodation:** Le Meridien, Beach Rotana
- **Dining:** Mall dining, hotel restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Cinema, malls
- **Best For:** Budget travelers, central location
- **Price Level:** $$–$$$

### Al Mina / Port Area
- **Vibe:** Traditional, fish market, heritage
- **Landmarks:** Mina Fish Market, Carpet Souk, Iranian Souk
- **Accommodation:** Limited
- **Dining:** Traditional restaurants, fish market eateries
- **Entertainment:** Heritage experiences
- **Best For:** Authentic experiences
- **Price Level:** $–$$

## 9. Local Knowledge

### Transport
- **Taxi/Uber/Careem:** Available
- **Metro:** Not yet (under construction)
- **Bus:** Extensive network
- **Taxis:** Safe, metered

### Alcohol
- Available at licensed hotels, restaurants, bars
- More conservative than Dubai
- Same 21+ age rule

### Dress Code
- More conservative than Dubai
- Cover shoulders/knees in public
- Relaxed at hotels/resorts
- Full coverage at mosques (abayas provided)

### Cultural Notes
- Capital city, more Emirati presence
- Sheikh Zayed Mosque requires modest dress
- Friday prayer times respected
- More family-oriented than Dubai
