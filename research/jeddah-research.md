## 1. Market Overview

Jeddah is Saudi Arabia's second-largest city, the gateway to Mecca, and the most cosmopolitan Saudi city with a Red Sea coastal identity. Known for its UNESCO heritage site (Al-Balad), beach culture, and more relaxed atmosphere compared to Riyadh.

### Key Statistics
- **Population:** ~4.7 million
- **Character:** Coastal, cosmopolitan, historic
- **Currency:** Saudi Riyal (SAR)
- **Key Identity:** "Bride of the Red Sea", gateway to Mecca

### Key Differences from Riyadh
| Factor | Riyadh | Jeddah |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Vibe | Modern, business | Coastal, relaxed |
| Heritage | Diriyah | Al-Balad UNESCO |
| Season | Riyadh Season (winter) | Jeddah Season (summer) |
| Climate | Desert, extreme | Humid, Red Sea breeze |
| Tourism | Entertainment-focused | Beach + heritage |

## 2. Areas & Neighborhoods

### Al-Balad (Historic Jeddah)
- **Vibe:** UNESCO World Heritage, traditional, authentic
- **Landmarks:** 600+ historic coral-stone buildings, 36 mosques, Nassif House
- **Dining:** Traditional restaurants, heritage cafes
- **Entertainment:** Night markets, heritage events
- **Best For:** Culture seekers, history lovers
- **Price Level:** $–$$

### The Corniche
- **Vibe:** Waterfront, family-friendly, sunset views
- **Landmarks:** King Fahd Fountain, sculptures, beaches
- **Dining:** Seafood restaurants, Red Sea views
- **Entertainment:** Beach activities, evening strolls
- **Best For:** Families, sunset dining
- **Price Level:** $$–$$$

### North Corniche / Obhur
- **Vibe:** Beach resorts, water sports
- **Landmarks:** Belajio Resort, beach clubs
- **Dining:** Resort restaurants
- **Entertainment:** Water sports, boat trips
- **Best For:** Beach seekers, water activities
- **Price Level:** $$$

### Al-Tahlia Street
- **Vibe:** Shopping, dining, modern
- **Landmarks:** Malls, restaurants
- **Dining:** International cuisines
- **Entertainment:** Shopping, cafes
- **Best For:** Shopping, dining
- **Price Level:** $$$

- **Vibe:** Mall-centric, cinema, entertainment

## 8. Local Knowledge

### Transport
- **Taxis:** Uber, Careem available
- **Driving:** International license accepted
- **Metro:** Under construction
- **Traffic:** Heavy, especially near Corniche

### Alcohol
- **Strictly prohibited** (like all Saudi)
- No bars or clubs
- All restaurants dry

### Dress Code
- More relaxed than Riyadh (coastal)
- Still modest (shoulders/knees covered)
- Beach: More relaxed at resorts

### Cultural Notes
- Gateway to Mecca (Hajj/Umrah pilgrims)
- More diverse, cosmopolitan
- Strong merchant/trading heritage
- Red Sea identity

### Best Time to Visit
- **Oct–Apr:** Cooler, pleasant
- **Jul–Dec:** Jeddah Season events
- **Summer:** Hot, humid (but indoor events)
