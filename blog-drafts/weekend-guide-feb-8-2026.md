## FAQ

**What is there to do in Bahrain this weekend?**
This weekend features world-class brunches at top hotels, live music in Adliya, shopping at The Avenues and City Centre, and family activities like the Bahrain National Museum and water parks.

**Where can I find Friday brunch in Bahrain?**
The best Friday brunches are at The Ritz-Carlton, Four Seasons, Gulf Hotel, and Sofitel. Prices range from BHD 25 to BHD 65 depending on the venue and package.

**What's the nightlife like in Bahrain?**
Bahrain has a vibrant nightlife scene centered around Adliya and Juffair. Popular spots include The Merchant for live music, Calexico for Latin vibes, and various hotel bars throughout the island.

**Are there family-friendly activities in Bahrain this weekend?**
Yes! The Bahrain National Museum offers free entry, Lost Paradise water park is great for all ages, and The Avenues has indoor entertainment for children.
